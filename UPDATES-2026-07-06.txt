HONG BADMINTON ACADEMY — UPDATES
Mon 6 Jul 2026

================================================================
PARENT APP
================================================================
- The child's DASHBOARD now leads with their MONTHLY SCORE (the coach's
  monthly marks, averaged out of 5) instead of the last exam result. The
  distracting "last exam /100" line is gone from the dashboard.
  (Full exam results still live on the Progress Card and the child's
  Training & exams card — nothing was lost, just moved off the front.)
- When a parent requests LEAVE, they can now propose a PREFERRED MAKEUP
  class. The list shows same-level upcoming sessions with how many spots
  are left; full sessions can't be picked. The admin still confirms it.

================================================================
COACH APP
================================================================
- COACH LEAVE is now easy to find. Each upcoming session on the coach's
  Schedule has a "Leave" button that jumps straight to the leave form.
  (It already existed but was buried inside the session page — that's why
  it felt missing.)

================================================================
ADMIN — LEAVE & MAKEUP
================================================================
- Pending leave now shows "Parent requested makeup: …" and the makeup
  dropdown is pre-filled with the parent's pick — approve to confirm, or
  change it.

================================================================
ADMIN — MONEY (WHO SEES WHAT)
================================================================
- BRANCH admins now see FOLLOW-UP payments only (unpaid + overdue) — their
  Invoices page is an "Outstanding Fees" worklist where they still chase,
  remind and mark paid. No revenue, no paid history, no analytics.
- Revenue, the analytics dashboard, and the invoice/payment exports are now
  SUPER-ADMIN only.
- New "COURT RENTALS" area (super-admin only): record what the academy pays
  to rent courts and see the cost per court, per month, with a CSV export.
  Add courts with an hourly rate, then log each rental (date / hours /
  amount / note).

================================================================
YOUR "DOUBLE-CHECK" QUESTIONS — ANSWERS
================================================================
- Coaches checked in to their own sessions? YES, already tracked. See
  Attendance → "Coach coverage" (last 30 days) and each session's "Coach on
  court" line. Coaches tap "I'm here" on their check-in board.
- Add a student to a session then scan their NFC card? YES — a coach can add
  an existing student as a drop-in and scan them straight away.
- Brand-new student on the spot? Kept ADMIN-ONLY (clean records — parent,
  fee, level all set properly). Coaches add existing students only.
- Assessment based on class? YES — the monthly assessment is entered class
  by class (whole roster grid), scored per student. The promotion exam stays
  per student (individual rubric) by design.
